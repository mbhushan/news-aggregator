NationStory Journalistic Writing Contest

In an effort to foster quality story telling, NationStory is sponsoring a journalistic writing contest in all major colleges across India.

Registration

Register to confirm your participation. Register here
Fill your profile details for better traction.

Eligibility

You must be enrolled in a participating university.

Guidelines

  1. Please choose any topic from Politics, Sports or Movies in India.
  2. Stories can be fact, fiction, prose or poem.
  3. You can write as many articles as you want across categories.
  4. Feel free to use pictures, videos, tweets to enhance your articles - but do give source credit and use copyrighted content with relevant permission. Only Single author stories qualify(no co-authored stories).

Awards

  • First Prize: Rs. 10,000
  • Second Prize: Rs. 7,000
  • Third Prize: Rs. 3,000

Important Dates

The contest begins on 4th May 2015 at 11:00 am IST and ends on 4th June 2015 at 11:00 am IST. Social scores would be considered for the duration of contest only.

Judging

Articles would be judged on social traction i.e. likes, comments, shares.
  • Like: 20 points
  • Comment: 30 points
  • Share: 10 points

Eg. if your article gets 100 likes, 100 comments and is shared 30 times from NationStory, then social score of the article is: 20*100 + 30*100 + 10*30 = 5300

Social score for a user is the sum total of social scores of all the articles he/she has written during contest window.

Users with highest social score will be selected as winners. Winners will be announced on 8th June 2015 at 11 am IST.
